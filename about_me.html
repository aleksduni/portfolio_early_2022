In 2004, My family had the opportunity to move to the United States from Eastern Europe through a visa diversity lottery. This mean't everything to us, it gave us another chance at life, along with the ability to flee poverty and government corruption, for this, I'm always greatful. This photo was my last birthday I was able to spend with my family in Albania.

Me and my brother had a hard time adjusting when we first moved to the United States, and the first step of that process was getting accustomed to a new school. This photo was on our first day of school.

After a couple of years in America, I started taking Martial Arts as a way to make new friends and I certainly did. This photo was taken after I had won 1st place in a local tournament.

In 2016, the first American born member of the family was brought into the world, my nephew, Daniel. This was a remembrance to those first steps we made on this land when we were the first generation of our family here.

I got my first job at Chipotle in 2017 and I managed to build a great set of relationships that I'll carry with me for the rest of my life.

In 2019, my family and I went back to Albania to visit all the people we had so dearly missed, this is a snapshot from that experience.

I made a stop at my hometown of Prenjas, Albania before returning to America.

After reaching my 20s I found an interest in traveling. This is a picture of my closest friends and I on a nature trip in Colorado.
